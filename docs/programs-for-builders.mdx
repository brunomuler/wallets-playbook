# Programs for Builders


Building on Stellar opens up opportunities to participate in various developer programs, accelerators, and funding initiatives. These programs provide resources, mentorship, and financial support to help bring your Stellar-based projects to life.

## Available Programs

### Stellar Community Fund (SCF)

The Stellar Community Fund (SCF) leverages community input to fund and support teams building on Stellar. Engage with a community-driven program that funds and fosters your project from ideation to successful launch and growth.

Check the website for more information: [Stellar Community Fund](https://communityfund.stellar.org/)

### Stellar X Draper University Founder Residency

Draper University has launched two accelerator programs in partnership with
the Stellar Development Foundation to support startups building on the
Stellar ecosystem. These programs are designed to impact up to 80 startups
over two years, focusing on boosting the Soroban smart contract platform and
AI integration.

Check the website for more information: [Stellar X Draper University Founder Residency](https://draperuniversity.com/stellar)
