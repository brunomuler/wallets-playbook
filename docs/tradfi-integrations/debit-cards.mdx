# Debit Cards

Debit cards are one of the most popular ways to provide spending access to digital wallets. Many companies provide card issuing services that allow you to create virtual or physical debit cards tied to your users' Stellar accounts.

These services typically support:
- Virtual card creation for online spending
- Physical card issuance with customizable designs
- Real-time transaction notifications
- Spending controls and limits
- Card freezing/unfreezing capabilities
